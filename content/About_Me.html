Hey — I’m Vishnu

I love using tech to solve real-world problems. Over the past 5+ years, I’ve worked across both B2B and B2C spaces — building products for restaurants in the US, AI/ML infrastructure platforms, leadership coaching solutions for enterprises, and helping global clients unlock value from data.

Beyond building products from 0 to 1, I enjoy scaling them — working closely with design, engineering, marketing, sales, and business teams to take ideas to market and drive impact.

I recently completed my MBA (PGPX) from IIM Ahmedabad — a transformative year of learning, reflection, unlearning old patterns, and having some incredible conversations along the way.

What excites me?
→ Building and scaling products from scratch
→ Working at the intersection of product, business, and customers
→ Solving complex problems with simple, human-centric solutions

Outside of work, I’m usually chasing good food, hunting for a great cup of coffee, or planning my next getaway. I read a lot — books, articles, random internet rabbit holes — and I’m always curious about how things work.

This blog is my little experiment with the internet — a space for me to tinker, write, and build in public.
